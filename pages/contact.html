Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?
Please use the contact form above and select "Report a Bug" as the subject. Include as much detail as possible, including your browser version, operating system, and steps to reproduce the issue.
Can I suggest new features?
Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Select "Feature Request" as the subject and describe the feature you'd like to see. We consider all suggestions carefully.
Is SimpleClock really free?
Yes, SimpleClock.online is completely free to use. We support the service through non-intrusive advertising.
How can I support SimpleClock?
The best way to support us is by sharing SimpleClock with others who might find it useful. You can also provide feedback to help us improve the service.
Do you have a mobile app?
Currently, SimpleClock.online is a web-based application that works on all devices through your browser. We're considering developing dedicated mobile apps in the future.
