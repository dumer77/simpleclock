Privacy Policy

At SimpleClock.online, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our online clock service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics, including pages visited, time spent on site, and general location (country/city level).
  • Preferences: Your language and timezone preferences are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) for your convenience.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Understand which features are most popular
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Remember your preferences for future visits

3. Cookies and Local Storage

  • Google Analytics Cookies: Used to track anonymous usage statistics
  • Local Storage: Used to save your language and timezone preferences locally on your device
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use advertising tracking cookies or sell your data

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements (when implemented). Google Ads Privacy

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access or alteration of any data. Since we collect minimal data and store preferences locally on your device, your privacy is inherently protected.

6. Children's Privacy

SimpleClock.online is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local preferences at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by using browser extensions
  • Use the service without storing any preferences

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
